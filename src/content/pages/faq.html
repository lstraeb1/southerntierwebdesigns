We usually spend around 1-2 weeks creating a design, which will then be presented to the client for approval, followed by 1-2 weeks to code the website itself.

For the lump sum package, you'll be charged half of the agreed upon amount upfront after signing the contract. The second half will be paid upon completion of the site.
